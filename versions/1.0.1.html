Fixes

- Campfires can only be build with 1 piece of their ingredient item.

Additions

- Campfire Logs can now be gathered by left-clicking a log/log-like block with an Axe on your hand while crouching.

- Players can now be turned into Campfire Logs by left-clicking onto them with an Axe, while crouching.
